About This Project

This is a Contemporary World educational project developed for GEC-9 course. Our interactive platform explores the complexities of globalization, international relations, and the interconnected nature of modern societies. Through comprehensive modules covering economic globalization, international law, regional integration, and global governance, we aim to provide students with a deeper understanding of contemporary global issues and their impact on our world today.
